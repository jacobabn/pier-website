— How this page reaches you —

You don't have to do anything.

If Pier is already on your Mac, every new version lands there automatically — you'll see it the next time you quit and reopen the app. Here's what happens behind the scenes.

01

We ship a new version here

A new Pier release goes live on this page. That publishes a small file our app knows how to read.

02

Your Pier notices, once a day

Next time you open Pier (or within 24 hours if it's already open), it quietly checks for new versions. No dialog, no prompt.

03

It downloads in the background

Pier grabs the update while you're working, verifies it's genuinely from us, and files it away until you're done.

04

Next quit, you're on the new version

When you close Pier, the new version takes over. Your connections, history, and licence stay intact.
