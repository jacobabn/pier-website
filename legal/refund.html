Refund Policy

Last updated 21 April 2026.

Short version: 14 days, no questions, full refund. If Pier doesn't work for you, email info@abn.company and we'll return your money.

1. How it works

  1. Email info@abn.company within 14 days of your purchase. Subject: Refund. Include the email you bought Pier with.
  2. We'll reply within one business day, confirm the refund, and process it to your original payment method via Stripe. Typical time-on-card: 5–10 business days.
  3. Your license key is revoked on the day of the refund. If you have Pier activated on 2 Macs, both go back to the paywall on the next license check.

2. EU consumer rights

EU consumer law normally gives you a 14-day right of withdrawal for digital products (Directive 2011/83/EU Art. 9). That right is lost only if two things happen:

  • You explicitly consent at checkout to immediate delivery of the digital product, AND
  • You acknowledge that by doing so you waive your right of withdrawal.

At Pier's Stripe checkout you can tick "Send me my license now — I understand this waives my 14-day withdrawal right." We recommend you do not tick that box if you want the full legal refund window. Either way, our own 14-day money-back policy above applies regardless — we match EU consumer law on the favourable side.

3. What we might refuse

Refunds can be declined if:

  • The request arrives after 14 days. (We'll still help fix any bug — that's a warranty matter, not a refund matter.)
  • We reasonably suspect the purchase was fraudulent or the key has been shared publicly.
  • You've chargeback'd the purchase without contacting us — we can't refund twice.

4. Bug refunds after 14 days

Even after the 14-day window, if you find a bug that makes Pier unusable for your legitimate use case and we can't fix it in a reasonable timeframe, we'll refund pro rata. Write to us — we'd rather fix it, but we'd rather refund than have an unhappy user either way.
